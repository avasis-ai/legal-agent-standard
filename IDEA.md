# LegalAgent-Standard (#15)

## Tagline
The legally binding open standard for autonomous contract agents.

## What It Does
This framework replaces informal SKILL.md rules with cryptographically signed, legally vetted execution bounds. It prevents agents from signing disadvantageous contracts by enforcing a strict, auditable liability chain.

## Inspired By
SOC2, AutoGen, Open-source compliance + Legal/Ethical edge

## Viral Potential
Directly addresses the most urgent existential threat to enterprise AI adoption. Chief Legal Officers and compliance teams will mandate its use before deploying agents. Standardizes a terrifying gray area of technology law.

## Unique Defensible Moat
Formal partnerships and continuous vetting by top-tier global law firms establish an insurmountable trust barrier. Unverified open-source alternatives cannot replicate the legal indemnification this standard provides.

/contracts, /audit-logs, Apache 2.0, dummy contract validation demo
